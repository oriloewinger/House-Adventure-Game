There is an old house on the end of a street in your town. Some say it belonged to a serial killer who tortured his victims within it's walls. Others say it belonged to a woman burned in the which trials. But those are just urban myths... Right? You and your friends decide to find out once and for all. You pack food, a flashlight, and your grandfather's old swiss army knife and approach the decaying mansion. A shiver of wind makes the hairs on your neck stand up. You are starting to have doubts. Will you...

Walk into the house... Run back the way you came... Run away, but take the short cut across the graveyard...
